DARK GLASS

A Design System from the Future

A minimalist design system inspired by space aesthetics, combining transparency, depth, and elegance through modern glassmorphism effects.

Philosophy

Dark Glass combines the purity of minimalism with the visual depth of modern UI patterns. Inspired by the infinity of space, it creates an aesthetic that is both futuristic and timeless.
